Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase my skills and experience as a web developer, and to provide a platform for potential clients and employers to learn more about me and my work.

Audience

The intended audience for this personal website is potential employers who are interested in hiring a skilled and experienced web developer. The website may also appeal to individuals and businesses who are looking to hire a web developer for freelance or project-based work.
